How to Play

→/D / GAS paddle — Throttle (in air: front flip)
←/A / BRAKE paddle — Brake (in air: back flip)
↑/W Lean forward (extra rotation in air)
↓/S Lean back (extra rotation in air)
Shift / ⤴ — Jump
Space / ⚡ — Boost (drains meter)
M Mute / unmute
R Restart run
Esc Pause / quit

On phone: hold the GAS paddle on the right, brake with the left, tap ⤴ to jump. In the air, the paddles also flip the bike — hold GAS for a front flip, hold BRAKE for a back flip. Land clean for combos and cash.
